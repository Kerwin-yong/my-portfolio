Overview

This project explores the design enhancement of a modern Fintech platform. I focused on transitioning legacy designs into a streamlined Figma-based design system, while optimizing user flows for payment processing and digital wallet management to ensure an intuitive and seamless experience.

My Role

I led the design refinement for this project, focusing on transforming complex data into an intuitive interface. My role involved establishing user personas, identifying pain points, and architecting a scalable Figma design system from legacy wireframes. By creating high-fidelity prototypes and optimizing user flows, I ensured a seamless and modern digital payment experience.
